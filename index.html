The challenge of balancing aesthetic appeal with functional clarity pushed me to think deeply about every typographic decision. This exploration of type systems and hierarchies showed me how minute details can dramatically impact user engagement.

Working across these different areas has taught me that great design exists at the intersection of technical skill, artistic intuition, and user empathy. Each project has been a building block in developing a holistic approach to design. Looking ahead, I'm excited by the endless possibilities for growth in this field.
